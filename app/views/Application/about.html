imgscalr.com (BETA)

imgscalr.com is a free, HTML5, Drag-and-Drop image scaling and hosting service.

This is what happens to your uploads:

  1. Image is given a unique name
  2. Smaller versions of the original are generated (e.g. thumbnail, small, etc.)
  3. Original image + all variations are uploaded to CDN
  4. Result page gives you direct links to the scaled versions

Reminder: All images uploaded are publicly accessible.

Please read the Terms of Service to better understand the rules for using the service (in short: be nice and don't do illegal stuff.)
